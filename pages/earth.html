Earth

Our Home Planet

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. About 29.2% of Earth's surface is land with remaining 70.8% is covered with water.

Astrology

In astrology, Earth represents the material foundation and grounding force of existence. As the center of our perceived universe, Earth embodies the element of stability, nurturing, and the physical manifestation of all other planetary energies. It's associated with the zodiac sign Taurus and represents our connection to the material world, home, and the physical body.

Alchemy

Within alchemical traditions, Earth symbolizes the prima materia or "first matter" from which all transformation begins. It represents the solid, stable foundation that must be present before any transmutation can occur. Earth embodies the qualities of patience, endurance, and the slow but steady process of growth and transformation that mirrors the geological processes that shape our world.
