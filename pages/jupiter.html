Jupiter

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System. It is a gas giant with a mass more than two and a half times that of all the other planets in the Solar System combined.

Astrology

In astrology, Jupiter represents expansion, wisdom, and abundance. Known as the "Greater Benefic," Jupiter governs higher learning, philosophy, religion, and long-distance travel. It's associated with the zodiac sign Sagittarius and embodies the qualities of optimism, generosity, and spiritual growth. Jupiter brings good fortune and represents our quest for meaning and understanding.

Alchemy

Within alchemical traditions, Jupiter symbolizes the principle of expansion and the element of Air. It represents the process of sublimation, where matter is elevated to a higher state. Jupiter embodies the transformative power of knowledge and wisdom, representing the alchemical stage where understanding leads to spiritual elevation and the expansion of consciousness.

Astronomy

Jupiter is a massive gas giant composed primarily of hydrogen and helium. Its iconic Great Red Spot is a persistent storm system larger than Earth. Jupiter has at least 79 known moons, including the four Galilean moons: Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto, which were discovered by Galileo in 1610.
