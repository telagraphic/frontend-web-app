Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System, being larger than only Mercury. Mars is often called the 'Red Planet' due to its reddish appearance, which is distinctive among the astronomical bodies visible to the naked eye.

Astrology

In astrology, Mars represents energy, action, and desire. Known as the "Red Planet," Mars governs courage, aggression, passion, and the drive to achieve goals. It's associated with the zodiac signs Aries and Scorpio, embodying qualities of leadership, determination, and the warrior spirit. Mars represents our primal instincts and the force that drives us to take action and overcome obstacles.

Alchemy

Within alchemical traditions, Mars symbolizes the principle of separation and the element of Fire. It represents the process of calcination, where matter is purified through intense heat and transformation. Mars embodies the masculine principle of action and willpower, representing the alchemical stage where raw materials are broken down and refined through conflict and struggle.

Astronomy

Mars is a terrestrial planet with a thin atmosphere composed primarily of carbon dioxide. Its surface features include the largest volcano in the Solar System, Olympus Mons, and the deepest canyon, Valles Marineris. Mars has two small moons: Phobos and Deimos, which are irregularly shaped and may be captured asteroids.
